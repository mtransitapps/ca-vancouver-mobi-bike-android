This application adds Vancouver Mobi Bike stations information to MonTransit.

This app contains the bike stations position & availability as well as the latest news from YouTube and @mobi_bikes on Twitter.

Mobi Bike is available in Vancouver, BC, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display bike stations information (number of available bikes & docks).

This application only has a temporary icon: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

This application is not related with Vancouver Bike Share Inc.

Permissions:
- Other: required to load bike stations information and to read news from YouTube and Twitter.
